About Paris

Paris, the capital city of France, is one of the most beautiful and historically rich cities in the world. Known as "La Ville Lumi√®re" (The City of Light), Paris is famous for its cafes, fashion, art museums, and monuments.

Famous Landmarks

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most recognizable structures in the world. It was built by Gustave Eiffel for the 1889 World's Fair.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre is the world's largest art museum, housing famous works like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame de Paris is a medieval Catholic cathedral known for its French Gothic architecture.

Culture

Paris has been a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. With numerous museums, theaters, and galleries, the city thrives in creative expression and history.

Cuisine

Parisian cuisine is world-renowned, from delicious pastries like croissants and macarons to exquisite French dining experiences. Don't miss out on trying French onion soup and baguettes while in Paris!
