About the Maldives

The Maldives, known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and luxurious overwater bungalows, is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean. It's a popular destination for honeymooners and travelers seeking a tranquil escape.

Famous Landmarks

Resort Islands

Experience ultimate luxury in the Maldives' private resort islands with pristine beaches and clear lagoons. Many resorts offer overwater villas for an unforgettable stay.

Snorkeling and Diving

The Maldives offers world-class snorkeling and diving with vibrant coral reefs, manta rays, and sea turtles. The clear waters make it perfect for underwater exploration.

Vaadhoo Island - Sea of Stars

On Vaadhoo Island, witness a magical natural phenomenon where the waves glow at night due to bioluminescent plankton, creating a "Sea of Stars" effect.

Culture

The Maldives has a rich cultural heritage influenced by various traders from the Middle East, Africa, and Asia. The locals, known as Maldivians, are predominantly Muslim and celebrate a blend of religious and traditional festivals.

Cuisine

Maldivian cuisine is heavily influenced by the sea, featuring dishes with fresh seafood, coconut, and rice. Don't miss the traditional dish called "Garudhiya," a fish soup served with rice, lime, and chili.
